Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 5, 2025

Thank you for using GlassApp (“we”, “us”, “our”). This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We designed GlassApp to be privacy-respecting by default: the Windows app runs locally and does not collect usage telemetry.

Scope

This policy covers (a) the GlassApp Windows application and (b) our website at glassapp.me (the “Site”).

Data we collect

  • License & activation (App): when validating or activating a license, we process a hardware identifier (HWID), license key or token, app version, basic device metadata, and IP address. This is required to issue and enforce licenses, apply device limits, and prevent fraud.
  • Purchases (Site/Payment): our payment provider (e.g., Gumroad/Stripe/LemonSqueezy) shares buyer email and basic order details with us so we can issue/manage your license and provide support. We never receive full payment card numbers.
  • Referral events (optional): if you use a referral link, we may log IP address and user agent for short-term attribution and abuse prevention.
  • Support: if you email us, we receive your email address and message content.

What we do not collect

  • No screen contents, no keystrokes, no per-window usage telemetry from the app.
  • No in-app analytics beacons. The app works offline except for license checks.

How we use data

  • Provide and maintain the service (license issuance, validation, updates).
  • Prevent abuse and fraud (rate limiting, chargeback/revocation handling).
  • Support you (answer questions, troubleshoot activation issues).
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting, regulatory requests).

Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Contract: to provide your license and the features you requested.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure our services and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: for any optional referral tracking or marketing you opt into.
  • Legal obligation: to meet record-keeping and compliance duties.

Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We share only what’s necessary with trusted processors, for example:

  • Payment processing (e.g., Gumroad/Stripe/LemonSqueezy) — purchase and licensing.
  • Email delivery — sending license emails and support replies.
  • Hosting/CDN — serving our Site and downloads.

Processors are bound by agreements to protect your data and act only on our instructions.

Retention

  • License records are kept while your license remains active and as required by law.
  • Referral/anti-abuse logs are kept for a limited period (typically ≤30 days).
  • Support emails are retained as long as needed to assist you and maintain records.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (TLS, access controls, server-side secrets). No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data and review our practices regularly.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries different from your own. When required, we rely on approved transfer mechanisms (e.g., SCCs) with our processors.

Your rights & choices

  • Access/Deletion/Correction: contact us to request access, deletion, or correction of your personal data.
  • Opt-out: you can use the Free app without purchase; you may opt out of optional referral programs or marketing.
  • EU/UK: you may have rights under GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection).
  • California: you may have rights under the CCPA/CPRA (know, delete, correct, limit use of sensitive personal info, non-discrimination).

To exercise rights, email support@glassapp.me. We’ll verify your request and respond consistent with applicable law.

Children

GlassApp is not directed to children under 13 (or the age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Do Not Track

Some browsers send a “DNT” signal. Because there’s no consistent industry response, our Site does not respond to DNT signals. We keep collection minimal by design.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll update the “Last updated” date and post changes here. If changes are material, we may provide additional notice.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email support@glassapp.me.
