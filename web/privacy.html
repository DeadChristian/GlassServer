Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

What we collect

  • Referral events: when someone clicks a referral link or downloads via that link, we log IP address and user agent to prevent abuse and match installs within 24 hours.
  • Activation & license checks: when the app verifies your tier (Free / Referral / Pro), we receive your device identifier (HWID) and minimal metadata (IP address and user agent) to enforce limits and fight fraud.
  • Purchases: payment providers send us buyer email and basic order details so we can issue or manage your license.

What we don’t collect

  • No usage telemetry (no screen content, no window titles, no keystrokes).
  • No analytics beacons on the app. The website is simple static files.

How we use data

  • Provide the service (license issuance, verification, referral attribution).
  • Prevent abuse (rate limiting, fraud checks, chargeback revocation).
  • Support (reply to your emails and assist with activation).

Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We share only what’s necessary with payment processors (e.g., Gumroad/Stripe/LemonSqueezy) and email delivery (your email address for license mail).

Retention

Referral and license-check logs are kept for a limited period to prevent abuse. Purchase records and license data are kept as long as needed to provide your license.

Your choices

  • You can use the Free version without purchase.
  • You can request access or deletion of email-bound data by contacting support. If we delete license data tied to an active license, the license may stop working.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (TLS, limited access, server-side secrets). No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll update the “Last updated” date above and post changes here.
